Japanese Tea Garden

The Japanese Tea Garden is a great spot to hike, to have a small picnic, have photoshoots, or if you just want to walk around and take a look at all the fairlytale-like scenery. There was also this cute tea place where you can take a break and pretend like your are in a mOvIeEeE. The only thing I would complain about are the HUGE bugs that fly around. If you can handle huge, creepy, blue beetles then this is the place for you.

San Antonio River Walk

The San Antonio is such a pretty placeeeee. You can walk along the side of the river, visit one of their many restaurants, take a ride on their boat tours, or shop from the tiny stores along the river. It was extremely hot and humid that day so I recommend you come prepared so that you get no sunburns or get dehydrated. Also, there are many cute ducks there.

My rating is.........
